Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee. Free shipping is available on orders over ₹2,000.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship across all of India. We're working on expanding to international destinations soon.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI, Net Banking, and digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay).

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After that, orders are processed and cannot be changed. Please contact customer service for assistance.

What if my item arrives damaged?

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund, including shipping costs.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Gift wrapping is available at checkout for an additional ₹99. We'll wrap your items beautifully and include a personalized message.

Still have questions? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!
